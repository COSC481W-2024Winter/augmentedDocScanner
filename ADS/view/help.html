Augmented Document Scanner Help

How to use the Augmented Document Scanner:

Step 1a: Using the upload button, upload one or more images of your Augmented Documents to be scanned.

Step 1b: Using the Camera button, take photos of your Augmented Document to be converted to a .pdf

Step 2: Once photos are ready, press the Create PDF button.

Step 3: Download & use your new .PDF
